A Message on Responsible Growth

BM
Brian Moynihan
Chairman & CEO
Bank of America

In 2024, Bank of America demonstrated that being a responsible company and a successful business are not only compatible — they are mutually reinforcing.

Our Environmental score reached 94 out of 100, placing us in the 99th percentile of global banking peers. We have operated on 100% renewable electricity since 2019, achieved a 96.5% reduction in greenhouse gas emissions versus our 2010 baseline, and deployed $860 billion toward our $1.5 trillion sustainable finance goal — all ahead of schedule.

Our 213,000 teammates — 52% of whom are women — continue to be our greatest asset. We invested over $1.1 billion in training and development, raised our U.S. minimum wage to $23 per hour (with a commitment to reach $25 by 2025), and invested $380 million in our communities.

Responsible Growth is not a slogan. It is the discipline with which we run this company every day — for our clients, our shareholders, our teammates, and the communities we serve.
